Upcoming Events: Summer Art Exhibit

“The Magic of Spring”
by Nora Steele

Runs through Sunday Sept.18

Nora is a Naturalist for Wahkeena Nature Preserve and uses her illustration and photography to educate about the natural world. Her amazing art will be on sale and ready for pick-up at the end of the show. Proceeds will support the Wildlife Ambassador program at BNC.

Admissions:

$2.50/person or $10 for a family (family price is for parents and their children under the age of 18 or grandparents and their grandchildren under the age of 18)

Cash or check is preferred but can accept credit/debit card.

Admission covers the hiking trails, as well as the hands-on exhibits, nature shop, tree-top bird vista, nature library, Iddings Log House, and over 50 wildlife ambassadors!
