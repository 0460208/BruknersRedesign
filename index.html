Featured Nature Shop Item!


Polymer Clay Jewelry by Lilian Nichols

The artist of the month is Lilian Nichols, who works with polymer clay to make all sorts of jewelry - earrings, necklaces and other items. For her, polymer clay is like three dimensional paints – a perfect combination of her first two loves – painting and ceramics.

Always a nature lover, her art is inspired by colors, forms and patterns found in the natural world, sometimes with a touch of whimsy. She has been working in polymer clay for about thirty years, and also teaches, spreading the fun!

Jewelry prices range from $15 – $45
A percentage of all sales will be donated back to BNC!
